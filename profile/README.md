**Generative AI & Data‑Driven Business Intelligence**

## 🌟 What We Do

Blink Analytics delivers a mobile-first, privacy-centered analytics platform for employee and internal-communications intelligence. We empower organizations to:

- **Monitor platform usage, content reach, sentiment, and feedback**—across posts, surveys, and chats, filtered by team, geography, or campaign :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- **Track employee engagement, sentiment, and attrition risk** through targeted pulse surveys and predictive scoring :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  
- **Distribute insights with gamified dashboards and leaderboards** directly in-app to drive adoption and healthy competition :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
- **Preserve individual privacy via aggregated analytics**, with no exposure to individual-level data :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

---

## 📊 Core Features

- **Real‑Time Analytics Dashboard**: Shows metrics like reach, impressions, clicks, sentiment, and survey responses  
- **Granular Filters**: Segment by department, location, role, or campaign  
- **Generative AI “Blink Assist”**: AI-powered writing assistant that helps draft internal comms and surveys  
- **Automated Feedback**: Scheduled pulse surveys and check-ins with real-time insight delivery  
- **Gamification & Distribution**: Leaderboards, benchmarks, and personalized nudges to boost engagement  
- **Secure Integration**: SSO, integrations with HR systems, chat, forms, recognition, and community tools  
- **Mobile‑First Social Hub**: News feed, communities, chat, digital forms—all accessible on mobile
